About Me

I currently lead the Aerodynamics Analysis Group within the Naval Surface Warfare Center, Dahlgren Division. This team specializes in using computational fluid dynamics tools to evaluate the aerodynamic performance of various shapes of interest to the Navy.

I am also perusuing an Ph.D. degree in the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department at Old Dominion University. My research interests include physics-informed machine learning and data-augmented computational fluid dynamics. My previous eduational experiences include a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Mechanical Engineering (2014) and a Master's of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering (2015), both from the University of Connecticut.
